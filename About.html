About Glistron

Founded in August 2021 and is based in Yaounde, Cameroon, Glistron is a trusted marketplace for people to list, shop, and find products that is suiting to them. Having seen the growth in the e-commerce sector, We deemed it necessary to build a platform for our fellow Cameroonians to showcase their goods/products and make it easy to find buyers. Alongside those wanting such goods to find them with ease.
